A living letter
to your kids.
LifePage gives you 12 chapters — from your family roots and childhood neighborhood, all the way to the wisdom and philosophy you want your children to carry into their own lives. You don't have to fill them all at once. Even a few words in each chapter is a gift they'll have forever.
✦
Stuck? Let AI ask
the questions.
Every chapter has an AI Interview button. Tap it and the AI will start a warm, thoughtful conversation — asking you follow-up questions to pull out the details, stories, and feelings that make your chapter come alive. When you're done, your answers drop right into your chapter with one tap.
🎙
Talk it out,
don't type it out.
Rather speak than type? Hit Record and narrate your memories out loud. With a Groq API key (free to get), your voice is transcribed automatically into the chapter. On mobile, your keyboard's built-in dictation works great too — just tap the mic on your keyboard.
🔄
Memory doesn't move
in straight lines.
Writing about your career and suddenly remember a childhood story? Just change the Context Routing selector to the right chapter before saving. LifePage parks that memory safely and the AI will weave it into the right place at compile time.
🔐
Keep some chapters
just for you.
Toggle any chapter to 🔒 Vault mode to keep it private. When you compile in Abbreviated mode, those chapters are completely hidden from the output — but still preserved here for whenever you're ready to share them.
💾
Back up your story
regularly.
Your story is saved automatically in this browser, but browsers can be cleared. Use ⚙️ Settings → Download Backup to save a file to your computer or cloud storage. LifePage will also remind you to download a backup as you add content.

Quick Tips

No chapter needs to be complete. A few sentences is more than most people's kids will ever have. Come back and add more whenever you feel like it.

The AI Interview is the easiest way to fill a chapter — just answer questions naturally and let it pull the story out of you.

Compile turns all your chapters into a beautifully written first-person autobiography, ready to print or share.

About LifePage

Built to preserve your story, your voice, and your values — for the people who will carry them forward. Everything runs privately in your own browser. Nothing is sent anywhere without your action.
